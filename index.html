About Us

Founded in 2023 in Iceland by specialists in computer vision, robotics, and machinery for the fishing industry, Ration is taking aquaculture to the next level with groundbreaking automation technologies. Our flagship products, RationFeed and RationOva, are set to transform fish farming and salmon egg packaging. Driven by a commitment to efficiency and environmental stewardship, Ration is paving the way towards a more sustainable future in aquaculture.
